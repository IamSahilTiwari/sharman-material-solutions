Who We Are

Sharman Material Solutions is a Melbourne‑based supplier of premium building materials. Our team is dedicated to providing high‑quality products for construction, landscaping and drainage projects. We pride ourselves on reliability, fast delivery and exceptional customer service.

With a minimum order requirement of 4 tonnes or 4 cubic metres, we cater to both small and large projects. Our same‑day or next‑day delivery service ensures your materials arrive when you need them, keeping your projects on schedule and within budget.

Our mission is to support the growth and success of Melbourne’s building and landscaping communities by supplying materials that meet the highest industry standards. Whether you’re building a driveway, creating a garden or managing a construction site, we’re here to help.
